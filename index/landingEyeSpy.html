Adventure

As a child, your father was consumed with disocvering an ancient treasure known as the Siempre Stone, thought to be one of the largest a most pure crystals in the world. Legend has it that glows when held and grants one eternal life. To the natives, the treasure was thought to be cursed, so was hidden soon after it was disocvered. The natives also speak of a group of warriors who guard the crystal. These warriors are known as, "The Eternals" and are said to be the same ones who have protected the treasure all this time.

Your father, desperate to seek out the truth of the legend chose to venture out to find the stone, only to never to return.

Many years later, believing your father is still alive, you decide to follow where his trail ends. You are on a mission to find your father and retrieve the Siempre Stone. Your mother disapproves of your quest and forbids you from entering your father's study, where invaliable clues as to his whereabouts are surley hidden.
Your adventure consist of three phases:

  • Phase 1: Break into your fathers old study and locate his map before your mom sees you.

  • Phase 2: Follow the map to the treasure without dying.

  • Phase 3: Bring dad and the tresaure back home.
